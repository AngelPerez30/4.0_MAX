In this component only asks for two data: Sales expense, administration expense.
Sales expense


Those originated by sales or that are made for the promotion of these, such as: commissions to agents and their travel expenses, cost of samples and exhibitions, propaganda expenses, mail services, telephone and telegraph sales area, etc. .


Administration expense.


Administrative expenses are those incurred in the control and direction of an organization, but not directly identifiable with financing, marketing, or production operations.
