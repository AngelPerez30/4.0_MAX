Production cost components


  • Prime cost = raw material + direct labor.

  • Cost of transformation = direct labor + indirect costs

  • Cost of production = prime cost + indirect costs.

  • Operating expenses = distribution expenses + administrative expenses + financing expenses

  • Total cost = production cost + operating expenses.

  • Sales price = total cost +% of desired utility
